Wounds

    We, people have wounds. The wound is not only a physical injury but also a deep emotional pain or psychological trauma. Whenever we feel our wounds will be dehisced,we tend to inflict pain to others by dehisceing their wounds in the process of defending our wounds. We can see this everywhere in our day to day life , sometimes when we point out someone's mistake or when we criticize someone they tend to critisize us back, even if it's not related to the topic of discussion.

    I have seen this in my life many times, it's one of the way many conflicts arise in our life. Whenever am in this sitaution I empathize with the person and I recall that this is beacause of the wounds that they have and things they wanted to wanted to hide or feel ashemed of showing it to world. And I myself also get raged when they critisize me but I quickly recall that this is because of the wounds. And I try to recognize the wound that made me rage form their critisism and try to heal it by accepting the criticism and rectify my mistake.

   I guess that's a way to heal our wounds and to stop the cycle of dehiscence. And I think people who have less wounds or people who healed many of their wounds are more emotionally stable. The process of healing wounds is a long process and it requires a lot of patience and self love. And it might involve understanding, knowing, and accepting oneself. This process is not but it's worth doing because it can lead to a more peacful and emotionally stable life.
